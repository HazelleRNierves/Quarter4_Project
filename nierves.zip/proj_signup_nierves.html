Earth Hour

Earth Hour is a campaign for taking actoion on climate change for the good of everyone.initiated by the World Wide Fund for Nature, partnership with The Sydney Morning Herald. It is done by participants turning their lights off from 8:30 p.m. to 9:30 p.m. local time to reduce carbon emissions and light pollution in the environment.This is usually done in March for each year
